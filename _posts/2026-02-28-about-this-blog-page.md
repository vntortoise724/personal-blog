This weblog is used to share my knowledge of the world, or maybe not, because I am not that talented. You can read about my curiosity on the [About](/personal-blog/about/) page.

I will present my blogs into 3 + 1 collections, which are the 3 languages I am always practising: Vietnamese, English and Chinese. The last collection will be the combination of 2 or all the languages, and I will obviously call it “Multi-lingual”. 🤪

**VIETNAMESE**: I will use this language to write blogs about my discoveries, practices and POVs in Vietnamese culture and history. The blogs will then be translated into English, if I am free enough to do that. Vietnamese will also be used for some Chinese and Sino-Viet (Hán-Nôm) studies.

**ENGLISH**: I will try to write it in British style. Why? Because I feel elegant when using it. 🍵 This will also be **my major language** to communicate your comments (if you want to do that) and most of the blog writing.

**CHINESE**: It will mostly be used for my medieval Chinese, poems and cultural study. I will plan for proper Mandarin and Cantonese lessons in the future when I get richer. 🤑
I will have another page built for creative writing to try forming achieved ideas from this weblog into fantasies or fictional stories. Furthermore, as a developer, interactive experiences are also listed. 🗒️  

You can check my [Collection](/personal-blog/categories/) page to find my blogs swiftly.

Many thanks for reading this madman’s blogs!!!
